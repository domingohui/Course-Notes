1 Simple Harmonic Motion

Useful Formula
$\omega=\frac{\Delta\theta}{\Delta t}=\frac{2\pi}{T}=2\pi f$
$x=x_0\cos(\omega t)$ describes the object that is at $x=x_0$ at $t=0$.
